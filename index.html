Egypt-Japan University of Science and Technology (E-JUST)

E-JUST is a research university with the ambition of cultivating an academic environment and becoming a benchmark for education in Egypt and African countries. It was first established as a bilateral agreement project between the Egyptian and Japanese governments in May 2009. In 2010, it was ready to accept its first batch of students, making the dream a reality.

Who Are We?

IEEE SIGHT E-JUST Student Branch

IEEE SIGHT E-JUST Student Branch Affinity Group is an affinity group committed to addressing humanitarian challenges by leveraging science, engineering, and technology. Its mandate is to build a more informed and empowered community by promoting scientific and technological awareness and collaborating with industries and organizations that share the same humanitarian vision.

IEEE Global Network

IEEE is the world's largest professional association dedicated to advancing technological innovation and excellence for the benefit of humanity. Through its highly cited publications, conferences, technology standards, and professional and educational activities, IEEE and its members inspire a global community.

Our Activities

Fire-Fighting Boats Robotics Competition

Organized to inspire innovation and problem-solving among skilled youth. Participants, from pre-university students to graduates, designed and built functional boats to put-down fire samples. Supported by sponsors like NTRA, Cintech, and E-JUST.

International Japan-Africa Conference (JAC-ECC 2024)

JAC-ECC is an annual conference uniting researchers, primarily from Egypt and Japan, to exchange knowledge in Tech fields. IEEE E-JUST Student Branch has supported its organization for the past two years and will continue in 2026.

Bio-Vision 2024

IEEE E-JUST Student Branch held Bio-Vision Event on September 25, 2024, focusing on biomedical engineering innovations with expert-led sessions and cultural activities like origami and kimono dressing, providing networking opportunities and career development insights.

Our Vision

"Advancing technology for the benefit of humanity" ‚Äî this guiding principle of IEEE is at the heart of what we aim to achieve with TechRise Africa. Our hackathon initiative is designed to inspire and empower young minds to develop practical, technology-driven solutions to the pressing humanitarian challenges facing the African continent. By leveraging humanitarian technology, we seek to channel the creativity, innovation, and energy of youth into real-world impact.


Our core mission is to nurture the ideas of young innovators and support them in evolving their visions into tangible solutions. We believe that young people are not only the leaders of tomorrow but also the problem-solvers of today. Through TechRise Africa, we strive to contribute to IEEE's vision by enabling youth-led technological innovation that directly benefits humanity.


In addition to fostering innovation, we are committed to providing long-term support to outstanding teams. We aim to connect the most promising projects with incubators, mentors, and funding opportunities. This ecosystem will help participants move beyond the hackathon stage‚Äîaccelerating their solutions toward market readiness and real-world implementation across communities in need.


By bridging innovation with opportunity, TechRise Africa aspires to be a launchpad for the next generation of African changemakers.

Program Agenda

The TechRise Africa Hackathon is a multi-phase innovation challenge designed to guide you from a brilliant idea to a fully-realized project.

1

Team Registration and Onboarding

Your journey begins here! We're putting out an open call for passionate, youth-led teams from across Africa and the world to join us.

2

Mentorship Sessions

Once teams are registered, each is matched with a dedicated expert mentor‚Äîa professional from diverse fields. These one-on-one mentorship sessions are designed to provide personalized guidance. Finalizing this journey with initial screening through 4 minutes pitching video.

3

Hybrid Hackathon Day

This is the main event! To welcome participants from everywhere, our hackathon is a hybrid experience:

On-site: Local teams can join us in person for a dynamic, hands-on event.
Virtual: Teams from around the world can connect and compete online.

During the hackathon, you'll work intensively to perfect your pitch for our panel of expert judges. The top three winning teams will receive prizes, recognition, and ongoing support including follow-up mentorship, incubator connections, and support in funding and implementation pathways.

Community Partners

Join Our Community Network

We invite you to partner with us and help shape an event that will ignite the next generation of innovators. As a Community Partner, you'll play a vital role in expanding our reach and sharing the message of innovation for humanity with a broader audience.

How You Can Contribute:

  • Promote the Event: Spread the word through your network! Share our posts on social media, feature us in your newsletters, and help us reach aspiring changemakers.
  • Encourage Participation: Invite your community members to join the hackathon, offering them a unique opportunity to learn, connect, and grow.
  • Attend and Engage: Encourage your team to attend the event and be a part of the excitement. Your reserved slot will ensure your team has the opportunity to network and engage with participants.

Partnership Benefits:

  • Brand visibility on all event materials and platforms

  • Positive community impact recognition
  • Discounted tickets for affiliated participants
Technical Partners

Shape the Future of Innovation

We seek Technical Partners to collaborate with us in creating a highly impactful and educational experience for our participants. By joining us, you will play a vital role in shaping the future of innovation and humanitarian technology.

We Expect You to Participate In:

  • Mentorship: Offering one-on-one or group mentoring to participants during the preparation phase.
  • Judging and Feedback: Serving as judges during the competition and providing constructive feedback to participants to foster their growth.

Judging Panel

The judging panel will be composed of representatives from all key stakeholders involved in bringing this hackathon to life. To ensure fairness and inclusivity, we are committed to assembling a bilingual panel, with judges fluent in both English and French‚Äîallowing teams to pitch in the language they are most comfortable with.

Our Vision

"Advancing technology for the benefit of humanity"

This guiding principle of IEEE is at the heart of what we aim to achieve with TechRise Africa.

üéØ Inspire and Empower

Our Day initiative is designed to inspire and empower young minds to develop practical, technology-driven solutions to the pressing humanitarian challenges facing the African continent. By leveraging humanitarian technology, we seek to channel the creativity, innovation, and energy of youth into real-world impact.

üå± Nurture Innovation

Our core mission is to nurture the ideas of young innovators and support them in evolving their visions into tangible solutions. We believe that young people are not only the leaders of tomorrow but also the problem-solvers of today.

üöÄ Long-term Support

We are committed to providing long-term support to outstanding teams. We aim to connect the most promising projects with incubators, mentors, and funding opportunities. This ecosystem will help participants move beyond the Day stage‚Äîaccelerating their solutions toward market readiness and real-world implementation.

Who We Are?

üèõÔ∏è Egypt-Japan University of Science and Technology (E-JUST)

E-JUST is a research university with the ambition of cultivating an academic environment and becoming a benchmark for education in Egypt and African countries. It was first established as a bilateral agreement project between the Egyptian and Japanese governments in May 2009. In 2010, it was ready to accept its first batch of students, making the dream a reality.

‚ö° IEEE - The World's Largest Technical Professional Organization

IEEE is the world's largest professional association dedicated to advancing technological innovation and excellence for the benefit of humanity. Through its highly cited publications, conferences, technology standards, and professional and educational activities, IEEE and its members inspire a global community.

ü§ù IEEE SIGHT E-JUST Student Branch Affinity Group

IEEE SIGHT E-JUST Student Branch Affinity Group is an affinity group committed to addressing humanitarian challenges by leveraging science, engineering, and technology. Its mandate is to build a more informed and empowered community by promoting scientific and technological awareness and collaborating with industries and organizations that share the same humanitarian vision.
